About University

Guru Nanak Dev University was established at Amritsar on November 24, 1969 to mark
the 500th birth anniversary of Sri Guru Nanak Dev Ji. It is both a residential and an affiliating university.
In conceiving the future course of the University, the objectives enshrined in the Guru Nanak Dev University
Act 1969, emphasised that the new University would make provision for imparting education and promoting
research in the humanities, learned professions, sciences, especially of applied nature and technology.
